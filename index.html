It is a period of peace. Rebel team leader and recent University of Denver full-stack coding Bootcamp, Matt Roger, looks for new opportunities. At his current position, he launches highly effective stikes with mass email marketing.

Trained in the art of Full-stack development and strong with the creative side of the force, this rebel coder is looking for a new company to grow with and fight the galactic empire of mediocre web design.

Pursued by the Empire’s sinister agents, Matt races home aboard his starship, custodian of the plans that can save his people and restore creativity to the galaxy….
